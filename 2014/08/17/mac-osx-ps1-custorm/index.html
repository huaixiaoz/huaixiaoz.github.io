终端bash环境PS1

  • Mac默认的终端:

    PS1='\h:\W \u\$ ' =====> /etc/bashrc

    huaixiaozs-Mac-BookPro:~ huaixiaoz$ -

  • archlinux终端：

    PS1='[\u@\h \W]\$ ' =====> /etc/bashrc

    [huaixiaoz@archlinux ~]$ _
